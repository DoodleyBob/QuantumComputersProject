What is Quantum Computing?

Quantum Computing is an emergent field of cutting edge computer science harnessing Quantum Mechanics to solve problems far past the ability of Classic Computers.

Quantum Computing includes a range of disiplines like Quantum Hardware and Quantum Algorithms, even though it is still in testing many believe that Quantum Computing will soon have the ability to solve problems better then Super Computers!
