What are Qubits?

A Qubit or a Quantum bit is the basic unit of info used to encode data in Quantum Computing,
Qubits are kinda like the Classic Computers bits used in binary but can store more data than
that of a traditional bit, Qubits also vastly improve cryptographic systems, and perform very advanced computations that would take thousands of years
